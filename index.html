Bio

  I am currently a Ph.D. student at Purdue University, Department of Computer Science, working on Statistical Machine Learning and Learning Theory with Prof. Jean Honorio.
  Previously in 2020, I received my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science (with honor) from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), Qian Xuesen College. I spent 6 wonderful years at XJTU after direct admission from middle school to the XJTU Special Class for the Gifted Young / Honors Youth Program in 2014.
  From 2017 to 2020, I worked as a research assistant at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, XJTU with Prof. Xuguang Lan, Prof. Nanning Zheng and Dr. Hanbo Zhang on Deep Reinforcement Learning. I am also interested in and have research experience on Machine Learning, Robotics and Computer Vision.
  In the summer of 2018, I visited National Unversity of Singapore (NUS) for the Summer Workshop in the School of Computing as a Research Intern. In the Fall semester of 2018, I visited University of California, Berkeley as an exchange student.
